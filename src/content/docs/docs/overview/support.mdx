Datum support is provided by our founding team and core engineers via live chat and email at support@datum.net. You can also chat with us (as well as some awesome community members and open source contributors) on [Discord](https://link.datum.net/discord). Other resources include:
- [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/datum-cloud/discussions) - Feature requests and changelog notices
- [Events & Community Huddles](https://www.datum.net/events/) - Events and monthly huddle calls hosted on Zoom.

Customers interested in our Provider tier and related services have access to a dedicated connected Slack channel for direct engagement with product experts.
