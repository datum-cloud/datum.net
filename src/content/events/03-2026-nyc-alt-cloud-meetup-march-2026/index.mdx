About our speakers:

**Alexander Jung** is Co-Founder and CPO at Unikraft, shipping unikernels to the world. A PhD candidate at Lancaster University, he focuses on high-performance, network-optimized virtualization and compile-time systems wizardry. Formerly CIO at Adjacent Systems, he built secure platforms for UK law enforcement and government. Alex will chat about why fast start up times and lightweight isolation matters!
