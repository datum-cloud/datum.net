Join us for a first Community Bingo Night, kicking off at the former Marlboro College campus dining hall on June 2nd.  
  
The goal is to have fun, create connections with neighbors, and get back into the historic dining hall on the beautiful Marlboro Music campus. All proceeds will benefit the FTA (Families and Teachers Association) fund at Marlboro School.  
  
You can expect a groovy time, due in no small part to:  
  
\- Ty Gibbons' enthusiastic bingo calling and color commentary  
\- An "all Johnny Cash" soundtrack  
\- Chicago "drug through the garden" style hot dogs, NA punch  
  
$5 donation at the door, $1 Bingo cards. Individual bingo winners will take half of each game's pot, with the other half going (along with any other proceeds) towards the FTA fund at Marlboro School.  

Doors open at 6:30, Bingo starting at 7pm sharp.

Let's gather and play us some BINGO!
