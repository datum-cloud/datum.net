question: "What is Datum's relationship with Kubernetes?"
order: 6
category: "features"
---

Datum's control plane is built on Kubernetes Custom Resource Definitions (CRDs). You can read some of the background in [Scot's blog here](https://www.datum.net/blog/control-plane-for-modern-service-providers/). While you don't have to use k8s to use Datum Cloud, it will feel quite natural if you do. An important benefit for users is the insane familiarity that LLMs have with the Kubernetes codebase. It sure does help Claude have a more seamless and accurate experience!
