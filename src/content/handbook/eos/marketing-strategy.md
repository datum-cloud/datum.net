This tool captures (at the highest level) the customers we are trying to serve, why they might need to buy from us, and our process for getting more of them. 

## “The List”

- Demographic - Alt Cloud providers who are aggressively building for the future

- Geographic - English speaking “service provider” businesses targeting global audiences 

- Psychographic - Focused on automation and gaining an advantage

## Three Uniques

- We are 100% focused on providers and helping them win.

- We have the largest ecosystem of magnetic “alt cloud” providers.

- We have a holistic commitment to open source that engenders confidence & trust.

## Proven Process

1. Build a brand people love and trust through digital and non digital (events, speaking, community)

2. Launch new initiatives (including open source) with a cloud-first experience for ease of adoption.

3. Work closely with real clients and influencers to build elegant product experiences.

4. Communicate regularly to stakeholders, including a vibrant roadmap and public benchmarking.

5. Deliver value through a differentiated success-led motion that starts super early.
